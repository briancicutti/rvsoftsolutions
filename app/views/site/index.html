Agile <%= image_tag "icons/time.gif" %> We truly believe that Agile methodologies bring the most value to our customers. We don't want to project impractical hour estimates or leave customers waiting for months for a product they may not even love. Bite size chunks of work are defined and minimal solutions created so that no work is lost. This keeps the customers happy (costs are lower), and the developers happy (hard work not wasted).

Price <%= image_tag "icons/dude.gif" %> We have a fundamental belief that we want to provide VALUE for our customers. A lot of development shops are charging what the customer will pay, not their best price. We feel that our $99/hour rate for all services is both fair and affordable. We want to create relationships with businesses, not gouge them.

Web and Mobile Products <%= image_tag "icons/task.gif" %> With an increasingly remote and mobile workforce we truly believe that "cloud" computing is not just the future, but our very real present. Web applications (not websites) allow for enterprise grade software to be accessible to anyone, anywhere with all of your data kept in a centralized location where it can be backed up. No messy burden on IT; no more user error leading to lost information.
